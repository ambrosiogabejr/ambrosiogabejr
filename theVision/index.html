Over the past 2 years we've seen a dramatic rise in video viewing on all digital instruments plus the Data to target according to buying habits, these are built off 40 layers of data gathering. I now believe in the power of TV targeting people through multiple devices.

Constant change in Comcast, zone TV, reach and frequency across many networks, interconnect with other MSOs, placing ads on Xfinity.com, placing TV commercials on multiple products in the digital space. Our reportiore has changed dramatically over the past five years.
